Sia $\alpha:[a,b]\to\mathbb{R}^n$ [[Cammino|cammino]] continuo a tratti, $f,g:\alpha([a,b])\to\mathbb{R},l,m\in\mathbb{R}$, allora
$$
\int_{\alpha}(lf(x)+mg(x))ds=l\int_{\alpha}f(x)ds+m\int_{\alpha}g(x)ds
$$
