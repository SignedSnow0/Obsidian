Siano $A\subseteq\mathbb{R}^n,f:A\to\mathbb{R}^m,x_0\in D(A),l\in\mathbb{R}^m$, scriveremo
$$
\lim_{x\to x_0}f(x)=l
$$
se 
$$
\forall\epsilon>0\quad\exists\delta(\epsilon)>0:\forall x\in B_n(x_0,\delta(\epsilon)),x\ne x_0\implies ||f(x)-l||_m<\epsilon
$$
